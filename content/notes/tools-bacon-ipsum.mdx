Alright, here's the deal. Sometimes we all need a little placeholder text when building out our projects. There's tons of options out there but I just wanted to take a quick moment to showcase a nugget of internet gold that I ran into a while back. It is with great joy that I present, [BACON IPSUM](https://baconipsum.com/). A very... meaty... text generator created by [<small><FiTwitter /></small> Pete Nelson <small><FiTwitter /></small>](https://twitter.com/CodeGeekATX). If you need any convincing, Bacon Ipsum has even made its way into Google's [Responsive Design Documentation](https://web.dev/responsive-web-design-basics/#optimize-text-for-reading)!
